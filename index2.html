About Me

Erinna Dian Kristianti

Hi! I'm Erinna Dian Kristianti, a vocational high school student from Malang, East Java, Indonesia. I aspire to be a professional programmer and photographer, but I'm still in the process of achieving that.

I'm very happy if there's a photography competition because I've always loved taking photos of random things and the results are beautiful. I also enjoy editing photos and videos, and I enjoy listening to music because music is usually what calms my mind.

I was born in Surabaya on November 6, 2008, but I grew up in Malang.
